Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a creamy and flavorful Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's simple yet rich, and perfect for a quick, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 150g (5 oz) pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g (1/2 cup) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water and drain
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan, and black pepper.
  4. Quickly mix the hot pasta with the pancetta and then add the egg mixture, stirring well.
  5. Add a bit of pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, topped with more cheese and pepper if desired.
